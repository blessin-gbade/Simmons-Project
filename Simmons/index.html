My name is Blessing Gbadebo and I am a prospective international student. Here are a few reasons why Simmons should be anyone's first choice as it is mine. Please note that this website is inspired by the actual Simmons undergraduate websiteand I am honored to make this.

Ranking.

Simmons is ranked as one of the best schools in Boston. About 91% of Simmons undergraduates get employed or further their studies within six months of graduation.

If you are looking for a school with a small learning community, then Simmons is the place for you. The curriculum is customizable, and you would get to explore interests as much as you would like! Hands-on learning are also available and you would be trained with problem solving skills in a real world setting in whatever field you are in. Simmons is dedicated to innovative teaching and engaged learning which helps students in solving real life problems.

Simmons Honors Program

Simmons Honors Program is a cross-disciplinary program for all undergraduate majors designed to encourage students explore their majors and improve their knowledge by exploring other departments and programs. Members of the Honors Program are granted the opportunity to research programs, professinal coaching, commections to Honors alumnae/i and engage with the Boston community. Leaders in the Simmons community also have intimate meetings with Honors students to inspire and share their experiences on empowerment and leadership. Interested students can apply through Common App or Click Here.

Founder's Day

Every 30th of October, the Simmons community come together to celebrate the posthumous birthday of John Simmons, the founder of Simmons University. It is an important tradition at Simmons which is focused on community engagement, and I think it's one of the many things that makes Simmons unique.

..and yes, there's always cake(very important).

Simmons soiree.

Never got to attend prom(like me)? Well, Simmons has got you! Now you'll get the chance to make new memories with your college friends or go without a date. Either way, it's a great way to have an 'high school' feel in college and have fun outside of the rigorous class activities.

Residence Life

Adjusting to college life can be difficult. Good thing you won't have to worry about that at Simmons because Simmons residence halls are designed to be your second home.

Simmons Athletics

If you are into sports or cheering athletes on like I do, Simmons is the place for you. From soccer to hockey to swimming, there are so many options to choose from!

Aluminus Review

'I think the most prevalent culture shock for me was the sport fans. I had never seen more passionate sport fans in my life'-Lillian Farrell '18 Neuroscience and Behavior
