Introduction

My name is Blessing Gbadebo, and I am a prospective international student. I was inspired to build this website by the actual BC website. I love to code, and it is a massive privilege for me to make this. If you have any questions, please feel free to contact me. Here are a few reasons why Benedict College should be anyone's first choice institution, as it is mine.

Benedict Ranking

Benedict College is ranked among the top HBCUs in the Southeast and has a strong history of commitment to black Americans. Benedict is also named one of America's most innovative colleges.

Benedict Academics

Benedict provides an academic environment that encourages students to think critically and grow personally. It also prepares students for leadership roles in whatever fields they find themselves in. Benedict College also has Honors Program, which provides students with cultural enrichment and research opportunities. I love how BC encourages undergraduate research, as it helps to understand what you are learning.

Benedict Classroom

I love small classrooms because they make me feel like I am in a learning community, and I would be more comfortable being vulnerable and taking risks. At Benedict College, I would have the opportunity to work closely with my professors and receive the attention and support I would need to succeed.

Benedict Community Service

I love how Benedict College is committed to helping students engage more in their communities, such as service projects and volunteer work. Community service is a massive part of who I am because my parents have been a great example to me by consistently meeting the needs of our church.

Benedict Residence Halls

Being an international student means I won't see my family very often until I finish college, so being in a school that feels like home is vital to me. Fortunately, Benedict provides various on-campus housing options that help students feel comfortable in a supportive community for personal and psychological well-being. As a result, I am confident that Benedict College will be my home away from home.
